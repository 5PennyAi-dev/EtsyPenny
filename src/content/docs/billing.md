PennySEO uses a token system. Every AI-powered action costs a set number of tokens. You get a monthly token allowance with your plan, and you can buy extra tokens whenever you need them.

## Understanding tokens

Tokens are PennySEO's currency. Each action that uses AI processing or external data has a fixed token cost:

| Action | Cost |
| --- | --- |
| Import Etsy listings | Free |
| Analyse Design | 1 token |
| Generate SEO Keywords | 3 tokens |
| Re-generate SEO Keywords | 2 tokens |
| Generate Listing Draft | 1 token |
| Score Etsy Listing | 3 tokens per listing |

Your token balance is always visible in the sidebar. When it drops below 10, a warning indicator appears. If you run out of tokens mid-workflow, a modal explains your options: wait for your monthly reset or buy a token pack.

## Monthly quotas

Some actions use your plan's monthly quota instead of tokens:

:::deflist{icon="ArrowRight"}
- **Add custom keyword** — Adding a custom keyword to the Keyword Performance table. Each plan has a monthly limit on how many custom keywords you can add.
- **Add from Favorites** — Adding keywords from your Favorite Tags bank to a listing. Also limited by your plan's monthly quota.
:::

Quotas reset at the start of each billing cycle. You can check your current usage on the Billing page.

## Plans

PennySEO offers four plans, available in monthly or yearly billing (save 17% with yearly):

:::deflist{icon="ArrowRight"}
- **Free** — 15 tokens per month. Enough to fully optimize about 3 listings. Good for trying PennySEO and seeing results before committing.
- **Starter** — More tokens and higher quotas for sellers with a small shop. Ideal if you have 10–30 active listings.
- **Growth** — The most popular plan. Designed for active sellers who optimize regularly and have a growing catalog.
- **Pro** — Maximum tokens and quotas for high-volume sellers with large shops.
:::

All plans include the same features — there are no feature gates. The difference is purely in token allowance, monthly quotas, and Etsy import limits.

Visit the [Pricing page](/pricing) for current prices and detailed plan comparisons.

## Token packs

Need more tokens without changing your plan? Token packs are one-time purchases that never expire:

:::deflist{icon="ArrowRight"}
- **50 tokens** — Good for a quick top-up.
- **150 tokens** — Covers a batch of optimizations.
- **500 tokens** — Best value for heavy users.
:::

Purchased tokens are added to your bonus balance, which is separate from your monthly allowance. Monthly tokens are used first — bonus tokens are only consumed after your monthly balance runs out.

## The Billing page

Access the Billing page from the sidebar. It shows:

![Billing page showing current plan, token balance progress bar, usage stats, and transaction history — The Billing page — manage your plan, tokens, and view transaction history](/docs/billing-page.png)

:::deflist
- **Current Plan** — Your active plan with status badge. Click "Manage subscription" to open the Stripe Customer Portal, where you can upgrade, downgrade, cancel, or update payment methods.
- **Token Balance** — A progress bar showing how much of your monthly allowance you've used, plus your bonus token count. A "Buy tokens" button opens the token pack options.
- **Usage Stats** — How many custom keywords and favorite adds you've used this cycle versus your plan's limits.
- **Transaction History** — A paginated log of all token activity: monthly credits, pack purchases, and deductions per action. Color-coded for quick scanning.
:::

## Billing FAQ

:::faq
### When do monthly tokens reset?

At the start of each billing cycle (the day you subscribed). Unused monthly tokens don't carry over.

### Do bonus tokens expire?

No. Tokens purchased through token packs stay in your account until you use them.

### What happens if I downgrade?

Your new token allowance takes effect at the next billing cycle. Bonus tokens are unaffected.

### Can I get a refund?

Subscription management (cancellation, refunds) is handled through the Stripe Customer Portal, accessible from the Billing page.
:::
