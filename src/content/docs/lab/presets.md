Presets let you bundle up to 10 keywords from your [Favorite Tags](/docs/lab/favorites) into a named group that you can apply to any listing with one click. Think of them as keyword recipes — a "Funny Nurse Gifts" preset, a "Minimalist Home Décor" preset, a "Holiday Gift Guide" preset — ready to deploy whenever you need them.

## Creating a preset

Click **"Create Preset"** at the top of the Presets tab. A modal opens where you can:

:::deflist{icon="ArrowRight"}
- **Name your preset** — Give it a descriptive name. Duplicate names are blocked to avoid confusion.
- **Set context** — Optionally assign a Theme, Niche, and Sub-niche using the dropdown menus. This helps you organize presets by category later.
- **Select keywords** — Browse your Favorite Tags bank and check the keywords you want to include (maximum 10). Use the search bar and filter pills (All, Gems, High Volume, Low Competition) to find the right keywords quickly.
:::

A live summary bar at the bottom shows the aggregate stats of your selection: average volume, average competition, and average CPC — so you can gauge the strength of your preset before saving.

![Create Preset modal with keyword selection, filter pills, and live summary stats — Creating a preset — select keywords and see live aggregate stats](/docs/seolab-create-preset.png)

:::tip
Etsy allows 13 tags per listing. Presets cap at 10 to leave room for listing-specific keywords that make each product unique.
:::

## Managing presets

The Presets table shows each preset with sortable columns: name, keyword count, total volume, average competition, and average CPC.

Click any preset row to expand it and see the individual keywords inside, displayed as styled pills. From the expanded view you can:

:::deflist{icon="ArrowRight"}
- **Remove a keyword** — Click the trash icon on any keyword pill to remove it from the preset. Aggregate stats update instantly.
- **Add keywords** — Click the plus button to open a keyword picker and add more keywords from your bank (up to the 10-keyword limit).
- **Edit details** — Inline-edit the preset's name, theme, niche, or sub-niche directly in the table.
:::

![Presets table with an expanded preset showing keyword pills and aggregate stats — A preset expanded to show its keywords and aggregate stats](/docs/seolab-presets-table.png)

## Applying a preset to a listing

Click **"Apply to Listing"** on any preset row. A modal shows your existing listings with their thumbnails, titles, scores, and statuses. Select a listing and confirm — the preset's keywords are added to that listing's keyword pool.

Applied keywords go through the scoring pipeline (niche + transactional scoring) against the target listing's context, so scores may differ from what you see in the SEO Lab.

You can also apply presets from within the SEO Studio. In the [Keyword Performance table](/docs/studio/keywords), click the star icon in the header to open the Favorites picker, switch to the **Presets** tab, and select a preset. Its keywords are added directly to your current listing.

## Creating presets from the Studio

You don't have to build presets in the SEO Lab. While working on a listing in the SEO Studio, click **"Save as Preset"** in the Keyword Performance table header. Your currently selected keywords become the preset's starting pool — the top 10 by volume are pre-selected. The listing's theme, niche, and sub-niche are pre-filled as context.

Keywords that aren't already in your Favorite Tags bank are automatically saved there when the preset is created.

:::tip
After optimizing a listing that performs well, save its keywords as a preset. You can then apply that winning strategy to similar products instantly.
:::
